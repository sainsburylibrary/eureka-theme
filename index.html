About Eureka

Eureka is the Sainsbury Library's online repository for the Saïd Business School. It is based on EPrints and provides an online archive of the School's research and teaching output. Further details about the repository and its benefits for you are on the Information page.

A guide to depositing items with Eureka is available here.

Copyright Information

Before making the full text available through Eureka it is essential that you check the agreement that you have with your publisher. Publishing agreements often assign ownership of copyright to the publisher. However many publishers will now allow authors the right to make their publications available in an institutional repository in their author created, post-refereed version rather than the publisher's own PDF. However, you may find that your publisher has imposed an embargo period (e.g. 6 months) before your material can be made available on the repository.

The Sherpa Romeo website provides a list of those publishers that allow their PDFs to be used in repositories.

If you are in any doubt as to whether you have permission to submit the full text of your material in Eureka please contact the Sainsbury Library and we will investigate this for you.

The submission of your material to Eureka will not affect your intellectual property rights and Eureka will only make the material freely available in its original format.
